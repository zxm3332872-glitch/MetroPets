Bella

Cockapoo 2 Years Old Female

Bella's Story

Meet Bella, an incredibly sweet and affectionate 2-year-old Cockapoo! With her gorgeous wavy coat and bright, soulful eyes, she melts the heart of everyone she meets. Bella originally came to MetroPets looking for a fresh start, and she is more than ready to find a loving forever family.

Bella is the perfect mix of playful and cuddly. She absolutely loves her evening walks just as the sun sets, chasing after tennis balls, and sniffing around the neighborhood. When it's time to wind down, she is a professional snuggle bug who will happily curl up right next to you on the sofa for movie night.

She is fully house-trained, great with children, and gets along wonderfully with other dogs. If you are looking for a loyal, cheerful, and loving companion to brighten your days, Bella is the one for you. Open your home to her, and she will fill it with endless joy!
