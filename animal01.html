Bella

Cockapoo 2 Years Old Female

Bella's Story

Meet Bella, an incredibly sweet and affectionate 2-year-old Cockapoo! With her gorgeous wavy coat and bright, soulful eyes, she melts the heart of everyone she meets.

Bella is the perfect mix of playful and cuddly. She absolutely loves her evening walks just as the sun sets, chasing after tennis balls, and sniffing around the neighborhood.

She is fully house-trained, great with children, and gets along wonderfully with other dogs. If you are looking for a loyal, cheerful, and loving companion, Bella is the one for you!
