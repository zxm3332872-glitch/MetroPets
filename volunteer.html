Why Volunteer?

Volunteering at MetroPets helps provide care, companionship, and support to animals while they wait for their forever homes. It is also a great way to give back to the community, gain experience, and be part of a compassionate team.

Volunteer Roles

Animal Care Assistant

Help clean spaces, prepare supplies, and support daily care routines for shelter animals.

Dog Walker

Give dogs safe exercise, outdoor time, and positive attention throughout the day.

Adoption Support

Assist visitors, answer basic questions, and help create a welcoming adoption experience.

Event Helper

Support shelter events, donation drives, and outreach activities in the community.
